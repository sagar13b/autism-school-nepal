Our Story

Siddhartha Autism Care Center was established in March 2026 (2082/12/08 BS) to address the critical need for specialized autism services in the Rupandehi region.

To provide comprehensive, compassionate care and evidence-based therapies for children with autism in Nepal, ensuring every child has access to the support they need to reach their full potential.

Narayani Kunwar Adhikari

Founder & Director

Dedicated special education professional with extensive experience working with children with autism in Nepal.
