## Image Guidelines

### Format & Size
- **JPG** for photos (better compression)
- **PNG** for logos and graphics (transparency support)
- Optimize images before uploading (use TinyPNG.com or similar)
- Keep file sizes under 500KB each

### Privacy & Consent
**IMPORTANT:**
- Get written consent from parents/guardians for any photos of children
- Consider showing:
  - Activities from behind
  - Hands-only shots
  - Wide shots where faces aren't identifiable
  - Environments without people
- Blur faces if necessary
- Use stock photos as alternative

### Photography Tips
- Good natural lighting
- Clean, organized spaces
- Show activity and engagement
- Capture authentic moments
- High resolution (minimum 800x600px)

## How to Replace Placeholders

1. Prepare your image (resize, optimize)
2. Name it exactly as specified (e.g., `founder.jpg`)
3. Delete the placeholder text file
4. Copy your image to the same location
5. Test the website to ensure images load
